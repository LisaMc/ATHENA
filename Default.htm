What is ATHENA?

A portal for finding local experts and resources within the computational, biological, or clinical fields of biomedical research.

Who is listed in the database?

Over 400 STTR members specializing in solid tumor research, along with 400+ physicians and scientists in the area whose research focus involves "omics"

How do I edit my profile?

Use the "Profile" link in the navigation side bar and enter your name along with any information you would like to add or edit. Then simply send us this information, along with any other comments, via email by hitting "send".
