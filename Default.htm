Find Collaborators
Find local experts and resources within the computational, biological, or clinical fields of biomedical research.

Search a database of 800+ local experts contributing to the biomedical field, create or edit a searchable profile, generate printable reports of potential collaborators for large grants or projects, and visualize keywords, collaborative networks, titles or other designations by a variety of interactive figures.

Please email us at contact@STTRconnect.org with your suggestions, comments, questions, or details of profiles to add.
Patient Resources
Find help from a compiled, comprehensive database of no- or low-cost resources for cancer patients provided either online or located in Washington, Oregon, Idaho, Montana, Wyoming and Alaska.

Filter by resource type or cancer focus, search by key words, scroll through lists of online and location based resources, and even view places near you on a map.

Please email us at contact@STTRconnect.org with your suggestions, comments, questions, or details of resources to add.
